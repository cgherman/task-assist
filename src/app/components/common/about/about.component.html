This app provides you with a key feature that is currently missing in most task apps today - easy prioritization of your Google Tasks using a simple drag and drop matrix.
This tool combines the power of Google's Task framework with the proven strategies of Dwight D. Eishenhower and Steven Covey.
Use TaskAssist Now to Organize Your Google Tasks.
Your Google password is used to access your Google data, but your information is known only by Google. All calls are passed from your browser directly through Google's secure API channels.
AVG has published a video explaining more about proxy logins. As always, if you are using a public computer, do sign out and clear the cache when you leave.
